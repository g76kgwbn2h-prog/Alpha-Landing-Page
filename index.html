ALPHA

You're invited to Alpha.

FIND COMMUNITY & EXPLORE LIFE'S BIG QUESTIONS TOGETHER.

Alpha is a series of seven relaxed evenings of dinner and conversation where people of all backgrounds can get together to explore the big questions of life, Jesus, and faith. No pressure. No judgement. No charge. Just real conversations.  If you’re exploring for the first time, rethinking, or are just plain curious, Alpha is for you.

ALPHA LAUNCH NIGHT

Join this one-off Launch Night to see if Alpha is for you and Alpha then runs for seven Wednesday evenings.

So, what exactly happens at Alpha?

MEET UP

Grab a drink,  join your group of new friends, and spend some quality time catching up over some quality (free) food.

A LIVE TALK

Each week, there’s a 25-minute talk, from an everyday person, designed to spark conversation around the big questions of life, Jesus, and faith.​

GOOD CONVERSATION

The best part of Alpha. A chance to hear from others and share your perspective. You can say anything you want or nothing at all.

MORE OF THE SAME

If you’ve got time, you’re invited to carry on the conversation and hang out over a drink and some dessert. Lovely.

COME ALONG & SEE WHAT YOU THINK

Alpha kicks off with our Launch Night.

A fun, relaxed, informative evening to check out what Alpha is. Then if you like it simply come back the following Wednesday to continue the seven week Alpha journey of new friendships, fun, meaningful conversation and exploring the big questions of faith. We’d love you to join us!
